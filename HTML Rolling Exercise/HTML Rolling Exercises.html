Introduction

Introducing the mistake

In today’s digital world, ignoring cybersecurity is a costly mistake. Whether you're an individual or a business, leaving systems unprotected invites serious risks.

Since 2020, cyberattacks have increased by approximately 25% per year, driven by accelerated digitalization and the pandemic.

About

The consequences are too severe to ignore

Cyberattacks are growing more frequent and sophisticated. Hackers use phishing, ransomware, and data breaches to steal information, lock systems, and demand payment. Without cybersecurity, even a single click on a fake link can expose personal or business data. The damage can include financial loss, legal trouble, and permanent reputation harm.

In 2024, the average financial loss from a cyberattack in Canada exceeded USD $4.88 million per incident, marking the highest recorded to date.

Conclusion

You really need to take cybersecurity seriously

Cybersecurity isn’t optional. It’s a core part of digital responsibility. Investing in protection today means avoiding disaster tomorrow.
